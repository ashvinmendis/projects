Pet Feeder Robort

This robot allows you to feed your pets from anywhere in the world using your smartphone. Whether you're at work or on vacation, you can ensure your pets are well-fed and cared for without any distance limitations. With just a few taps on your phone, you can take care of your pets even when you're not at home. Also owners can set specific feeding times to ensure their pets are fed on schedule.

Specifications and Functions of the Robot:
  • The robot provides a facility to see the pet, allowing pet lovers to watch their pets' behavior remotely through the mobile application.
  • The user-friendly graphical user interface, available via Raspberry Pi or Android, enables control of the robot and the ability to save live pictures of the pets to monitor how much food has been eaten.
  • Controlled by a mobile application using Wi-Fi
  • Capable of moving both indoors and outdoors.
  • Providing a brief weather condition of the place.
  • Large food storage capacity.

The Pet Feeder Robot utilizes various sensors and components. Caterpillar chain, Gear motors, Motor drivers, Humidity and temperature sensor, Load cell sensor, Mini-camera, Raspberry Pi 3. These components work together to ensure the robot can move, feed pets, monitor environmental conditions, and provide real-time video feed to the pet owners.

Graphical User Interface (GUI) designed using Python and the Tkinter toolkit. This GUI allows users to move the robot, feed the pet, monitor food storage levels, and measure the temperature.

The PCB was designed by Eagle CAD and the robot's structure was designed using SolidWorks CAD software,This ensured precision and efficiency in creating the parts necessary for its assembly.
